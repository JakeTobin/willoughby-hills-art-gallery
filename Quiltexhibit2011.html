Willoughby Hills Quilt Guild

Our mission is to promote and perpetuate the art of quilting through education and preserve our heritage of quilting. To provide fellowship to people with a common interest.

Our current officers are:
President  Kathy Marsh 216-531-7949, Vice President Donna Cubic, Secretary Gloria Majeski, and Treasurer Barb Swyt

Community Quilts has donated over 100 quilts to needy causes in Northeast Ohio. They have made and donated adult, youth, and infant quilts to local hospitals, Hospice of the Western Reserve, Food Banks, children's homes, etc. They also made and donated a quilt to Quilt Pink - Quilt for the Cure.

 

We have a website with lots of photos of our quilts. Could you use these? Our website is www.freewebs.com/whqg. The website also has information about Community Quilts group which makes baby quilts for charity.
