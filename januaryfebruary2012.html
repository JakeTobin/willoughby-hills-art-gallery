MARTHA L. GERMANO

Mixed Media
Michael Nekic is Life-long resident of Northeast Ohio one of its treasured photophers.

His work is in the Cleveland Creates Show at the Beck Center in Lakewood,
The Regional Visions Show at Kokoon Gallery in th 78th Street Studios and in the Cleveland Artists Holiday Invitational
in the Trinity Cathedral Gallery in downtown Cleveland.

Awards
Honorable Mention in the 5th Annual International Digital Hall of Fame competition. Winning image was published in EFX magazine Winter/Spring 2004. This issue also reviewed the only art show (Bits and Pieces) ever staged at the Peter B. Lewis Building at Case Western Reserve University (CWRU), in which I had seven images.

Featured artist at NOTACON 2004. On the Board of Directors for The New Center for Art & Technology (NewCAT), a non-profit organization that promotes and educates the public on all forms of digital art.

Work can be viewed in the Cleveland Hopkins Airport’s Permanent Collection.

Featured in Focus magazine September 2006.

Appeared on WVIZ (PBS) Applause October 2006.

See more of Michael's work at
http://michaelnekic.net/#

Contact him at mikenekic@adelphia.net

A native of Cleveland, Ohio
Formal education:            attended the Cleveland Institute of Art
Pursued special studies:   Mt. St. Mary’s Collage – CA
Notre Dame College – OH
Imperial College – England

Studied with numerous outstanding instructors, and developed her
abstract contemporary style.

Subject matter:        Non-objective /non-representational/ abstract
Medium:                  Working in a variety of mixed water mediums /acrylic,
watercolor, inks, crayons and pencils and then           enhancing a watermedia painting with collage.

Memberships:          National League of American Pen Women, Inc
Society of Layerists in Multi-Media
Missouri Watercolor Society
Euclid Art Association
