John Davis

John Davis is an artist from Greater Cleveland working in watercolor, egg tempera and acrylic.  He is noted for his landscapes, seascapes and portraits. Before moving back to Cleveland in 2000, he operated Davis Pottery and Gallery in Chautauqua New York for 30 years.       
His works are in corporate and private collections.  Davis has exhibited in local and national art shows, recently winning awards from Gates Mills and Fairmount Art Shows and Euclid Art Association at Lakeland Community College.

DavisGallery.com

Bill Pappas is a Cleveland-based photographer whose work consists of scenic vignettes from a wide variety of locations, as well as studies of people from a visually enhanced perspective.
His work has been exhibited in the May Show at the Cleveland Museum of Art, The New York Art Director’s One Show, Photographis, Spaces Gallery, Dobama Theater Gallery, The Ninth Street Gallery, The Cleveland Society of Communicating Arts, The Butler Institute of Art,  NOVA juried shows, The Willoughby Fine Arts Association,  the Gates Mills juried shows, The Fairmount Center for the Arts, and The Center for Fine Art Photography in Colorado. Bill is a member of the Red Dot Project, The Euclid Art Association, and exhibits his work locally in the Juma Gallery in the Murray Hill Schoolhouse.
