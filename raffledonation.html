Raffle and Donations

Your help can make The Willoughby Hills Community Art Gallery “The Place Where Art Lives”. 

  The purpose of this gallery is to educate and promote the arts and the artists.   The art gallery was established with the help of the mayor and members of the city council of Willoughby Hills.  By supplying us the initial funds for a hanging system and the bare walls of the atrium of the Willoughby Hills Community Center, the galley was established. 


The gallery has been self funded through the sale of a poster of each exhibit and 20% of the sale of artwork.  It has also earned funds through a raffle held at every opening of donated art by artists or on occasion a framed poster of the exhibit. This is not enough to keep the gallery going or to grow.   

We are looking for help from our supporters and those who believe in the arts.

I know the importance of art and this gallery is one place where art lives and grows. 

100% of every dollar raised goes directly to funding these efforts.  There are no administrative costs.  The gallery is staffed by volunteer Gallery Directory and other volunteers.
