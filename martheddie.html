EDDIE MITCHELL

Authentic Oil Paintings on Canvas

A native of Cleveland, Ohio
Formal education:            attended the Cleveland Institute of Art
Pursued special studies:   Mt. St. Mary’s Collage – CA
                                         Notre Dame College – OH
                                          Imperial College – England

Studied with numerous outstanding instructors, and developed her
 abstract contemporary style.

Subject matter:        Non-objective /non-representational/ abstract
Medium:                  Working in a variety of mixed water mediums /acrylic,
watercolor, inks, crayons and pencils and then           enhancing a watermedia painting with collage.

Memberships:          National League of American Pen Women, Inc
                                 Society of Layerists in Multi-Media
                               Missouri Watercolor Society
                               Euclid Art Association

My Education & Career:

  • BFA – Cleveland Institute of Art - Graduated in 1987 (Major: Painting; Minor: Drawing)
  • Run a full time studio for the past 22 years
  • Part time college art teacher for past 17 years (Lakeland Community College) 
  • Represented in galleries in Ohio, Michigan, Pennsylvania, and Florida
  • Commissioned for various, perhaps even prominent public locations
  • Participated in over 135 art shows

My Painting Philosophy

I love the fact that light seems to engender a sense of universal & almost irresistible optimism.

It is this optimism that I am endeavoring to convey in my paintings.

I am also intrigued by the mysterious and immense energy of color;
Color helps to enhance this play of light and intensify this feeling of joyfulness. 

THANK YOU !!

Eddie Mitchell
