Western Reserve Photographic Society

http://www.wrpsphoto.org/

Northeast Ohio's Greatest Camera Club. Whether you're a novice, professional, or just enjoy the beauty of photography, this club is for you!
Techniques, demonstrations, and presentations offer new perspecitiives to the world around us. Competitions are designed for member participation. and the public is invited to watch.

We meet on the second and fourth Tuesday of each month (except July, August and December) at 7:00 p.m. in the Penitentiary Glen Nature Center in Kirtland,Ohio. No registration is required.

Join Us!

Our current membership consists of over 100 photographers from Northeastern Ohio. We're young and old, novice to professionals, and we all love to share our passion for photography. Come see what we're all about! If your interested in joining us, see our membership page!

The Western Reserve Photographic Society (WRPS) is a proud member of the Photographic Society of America!
